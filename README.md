# Customer-Retention-Dashboard-PowerBI
This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of customer loyalty and retention behaviour across the sales dataset. It is designed to help business stakeholders quickly understand who is coming back, who is not, and why it matters moving beyond raw sales numbers to focus on the quality and longevity of customer relationships.

## Objective
Analyze customer retention, churn, and high-value customers.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Excel

## Key Metrics
- Retention Rate
- Churn Rate
- Customer Lifetime Value

## Insights
- 65% customers are repeat buyers
- High churn in South region
- Top 10 customers contribute 40% revenue

##  Outcome
Helps businesses improve retention strategies and identify high-value customers.
